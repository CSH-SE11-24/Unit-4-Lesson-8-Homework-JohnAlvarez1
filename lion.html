Fun Facts About Lions

  • Lions are the only cats that live in groups called prides.
  • A pride can be up to 30 lions, depending on how much food and water is available.
  • Female lions are the main hunters.
  • While they’re out looking for food, the males guard the pride’s territory and their young.
  • A lion’s roar can be heard up to eight kilometres away.
  • An adult male’s roar can be heard up to 8km away.
  • A female lion needs 5kg of meat a day.
  • The name for a baby lion is a cub, whelp or lionet.
