Fun Facts About Tigers

  • Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world, weighing up to 363kg and measuring up to 3.3m.
  • Tigers have a distinctive orange and black striped coat, which helps them camouflage in their habitat.
  • Tigers are carnivores, eating mainly large mammals such as deer, wild pigs, antelope and buffalo.
  • Tigers are apex predators, meaning they have no natural enemies in the wild.
  • Tigers can run up to 65km/hour and leap up to 32 feet in a single bound.
  • Tigers are endangered, with only around 3,900 left in the wild.
  • Tigers can roar very loudly, and their roar can be heard from 3 kilometres away.
